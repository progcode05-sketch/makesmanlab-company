Our story

Built by someone
who uses LinkedIn.

Makersmanlab is a one-person software studio. Every product starts from the same place — a real problem I ran into while using LinkedIn myself, and couldn't find a good solution for.

Noxlin came from wanting to post consistently without spending hours writing. Linkora came from copy-pasting generic AI comments and cringing every time. Both tools exist because the existing options were either too bloated, too generic, or just not honest about what they were.

"I'd rather build two tools that do one thing really well than a platform that does ten things badly."
